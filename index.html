Aria · Shared mediator

Guidance for the conversation, not a side to take.

Aria joins a shared Talk only after both people accept. With the surrounding conversation in view, she can separate intention from impact, slow down a tense exchange, and help both people reach a useful next step.

  • Consent from both people before Aria participates
  • Neutral context that reflects both perspectives without choosing a winner
  • Light, Balanced, and Active involvement controls
  • Direct @Aria questions whenever either person wants help

Private Coach · Only you can see

Work out what you mean before the message leaves your hands.

Tell Private Coach what happened in your own words. It helps shape a clear, emotionally honest message while keeping your questions, rough drafts, explanations, and suggested replies private from the other person.

  • Reframe blame, defensiveness, or mixed signals without erasing your point
  • Build thoughtful replies and apologies with concrete next steps
  • Translate emotional meaning while keeping the tone natural
  • Review, copy, insert, or try another version; nothing sends automatically

Describe a vulnerable moment naturally, then create a message that carries the same emotion in your partner's language.

The translation protects meaning, voice, and cultural tone rather than swapping words mechanically.
